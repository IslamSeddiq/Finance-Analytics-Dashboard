# 🧠 About Project

A Power BI dashboard designed to give finance stakeholders a clear, at-a-glance view of sales performance, profitability, and discounting trends — built to answer key business questions

---

# 🔍 Project Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance and growth trends
* Analyze net sales by month, segment, and country
* Track progress against sales targets
* Understand the relationship between discounts and gross sales
* Evaluate product-level profitability (Sales, COGS, and Profit)
* Support strategic financial decision-making

---

# 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

* Power BI (Visualization & Dashboard Development)
* Excel (Raw Data Source & Exploration)
* DAX (Custom Measures & Calculated Columns)
* Power Query
* Business Intelligence & Data Visualization

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive KPI cards (Net Sales, Avg Order Value, Gross Profit, Sales Growth Rate, Discount Rate)
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Segment-wise revenue breakdown (Government, Small Business, Enterprise, Midmarket, Channel Partner)
* Country-level sales comparison
* Sales target tracking with gauge visualization
* Discount vs. gross sales correlation analysis
* Product-level profitability table with conditional formatting
* Interactive slicers and filters for exploration (Year, Quarter, Discount Band)
* Modern and responsive dashboard UI

---

# 💡 Business Value

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Monitor overall financial performance in real time
* Identify which segments and regions drive the most revenue
* Evaluate whether discounting strategies are actually boosting sales
* Spot products with weak margins before they become a problem
* Improve strategic planning using data-driven insights
